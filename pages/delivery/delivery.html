Enter a Sales Order

  1. Open ENTER A SALES ORDER.
  2. Click Action Button (3 dots)
  3. Fill in zip and push tap or click Route
  4. Select Route
  5. Click calender next to date
  6. View Schedule
  7. Click Clear to check different route
